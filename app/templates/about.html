About UpliftIQ

AI-powered decision system for smarter marketing & higher ROI

What is UpliftIQ?

UpliftIQ is an intelligent analytics platform designed to optimize email campaigns using uplift modeling. Instead of targeting all users, it identifies those who are most likely to respond positively to a campaign.

Problem We Solve

Traditional marketing targets everyone, which leads to wasted budget and customer fatigue. UpliftIQ solves this by predicting the incremental impact of a campaign — helping businesses target only the right audience.
